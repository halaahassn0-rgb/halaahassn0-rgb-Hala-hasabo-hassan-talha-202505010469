1.1 Define the problem statement
Create a Python program that automatically calculates a customer’s bill in a café based on the quantity of items ordered.
1.2 What are the Inputs?
Customer name
Quantity of Coffee ordered
Quantity of Tea ordered
Quantity of Sandwich ordered
1.3 What are the outputs?
Receipt showing customer name
Quantity of each item
Total amount to pay
1.4 What would be the typical process flow?
Get customer name
Get quantity of coffee, tea, and sandwich
Calculate total price
Print receipt
Display total amount
1.5 What are the constraints?
Quantities must be positive integers
Prices are fixed:
Coffee = RM 8.50
Tea = RM 6.00
Sandwich = RM 12.00
